Top Tourist Destinations

Taj Mahal, Agra

A symbol of eternal love, this white marble mausoleum is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Golden Temple, Amritsar

The holiest Gurdwara of Sikhism, the Golden Temple is known for its stunning golden architecture and spiritual aura.

Mysore Palace, Karnataka

A royal residence of the Wodeyar dynasty, this Indo-Saracenic palace lights up beautifully at night and during Dussehra celebrations.

Kashi Vishwanath, Varanasi

One of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, located on the banks of the holy Ganges River.
